  
Interactive Design Process Timeline

For my portfolio punch-up, I’m creating an interactive Design Process Timeline that shows how my projects move from early research to final design. This timeline will be a stand alone section within my existing case studies, I have uploaded a low-fi of the Joey's timeline. It breaks the workflow into clear, easy to follow stages and gives viewers a better sense of how the project evolved over time.

This punch up focuses on showing process and reasoning.  
My current portfolio highlights outcomes, this addition helps explain the process.

By adding this guided walkthrough of my design process, viewers can better understand how and why decisions were made not just what the final result looks like.  
This will be an embedded interaction, not a separate portfolio entry, and will be reused across multiple projects to create consistency and a stronger overall narrative throughout my portfolio.
